No skill may be higher than 10 however. Skills numbers can be mixed any way you want, as long as the total adds up to 40. If you are a human, then this number is 42.


Ex: If Strength is 5, and so are all the other skills, then added up they make 35. That means you can add 5 more points to any of the skills, or 7 more points if you're human.
